About Me

Hi, I'm Shifa.

"Technical enough to build it. Bold enough to lead it. Driven enough to change it."

Computer Science student specialising in Data Science. Builder. IEEE leader. L'Oréal Women in Science Scholar.

My work lives at the intersection of AI and real-world impact. MaatriWatch — my AI-powered maternal health wristband — won ₹30,000 at Hackatly 4.0 and monitors postpartum vitals where healthcare is hardest to reach.

I lead IEEE MACE WIE as Chair and serve as Community Coordinator at IEEE RAS Kerala Section. I've organised technical events, mentored peers, and spoken at the L'Oréal Young Women in Science Scholars Conclave in New Delhi.

Started early. Interned at Hyasta Innovations in my first year. Still going.
